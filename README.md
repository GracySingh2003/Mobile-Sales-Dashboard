## 📊 Project Title:
Motorola Sales Analytics Dashboard – Mobile Sales Performance (Power BI)

## 📝 Project Overview:  
This project presents an **interactive mobile sales analytics dashboard** built using Power BI.  
It provides insights into sales performance, customer transactions, payment behavior, and product trends across different cities and brands.

## 🎯 Purpose:  
- Analyze mobile sales performance across regions  
- Identify top-performing brands and models  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Track monthly and weekly sales trends  
- Enable data-driven business decisions

## 🛠️ Tech Stack  
- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development  
- 🔄 **Power Query** – Data cleaning & transformation  
- 📐 **DAX** – Measures & KPI calculations  
- 📁 **Excel / CSV** – Data source

## 📂 Data Source:
- Mobile sales dataset including city, brand, model, payment methods, transactions, and ratings. 
- Developed through an **online Power BI course (SkillCourse – project-based learning)**

## 🚀 Features & Highlights:  

### 🔍 Business Problem:  
Mobile retail companies often struggle to analyze large datasets to understand regional performance, customer behavior, and product trends, leading to inefficient decision-making.

### 🎯 Goal: 
To build an **interactive dashboard** that provides actionable insights into mobile sales performance and helps businesses optimize their strategies.

### 📈 Key Metrics:  
- 💰 Total Sales: **769M**  
- 📦 Total Quantity: **19K**  
- 💳 Transactions: **4K**  
- 📊 Average Sales: **40K**

### 📊 Key Visuals: 
- 📌 KPI Cards (Sales, Quantity, Transactions, Average)  
- 🗺️ Sales by City (Map Visualization)  
- 📉 Monthly Quantity Trend (Line Chart)  
- 📊 Customer Ratings Distribution (Funnel Chart)  
- 🍩 Transactions by Payment Method (Pie Chart)  
- 📋 Brand Performance Table  
- 📊 Sales by Mobile Model (Bar Chart)  
- 📉 Sales by Day Name (Line Chart)  
- 🎛️ Filters (Month, Brand, Model, Payment Method)

### ⭐ Key Features:  
- Interactive filtering across multiple dimensions  
- Geographic sales analysis  
- Customer behavior insights  
- Time-based trend analysis  
- Clean and structured dashboard design

## 💡 Key Insights:  
-  Sales are concentrated in major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Hyderabad  
-  Digital payments dominate transactions  
-  Top models (iPhone SE, OnePlus, Galaxy series) drive revenue  
-  Monthly trends show fluctuations indicating seasonal demand  
-  Customer ratings are mostly positive (4–5 range)
